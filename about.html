About Us

Founded in 2007 by Jonas Rothschild, our restaurant is a Chicago institution that has been serving up delicious food and warm hospitality for over a decade. At our restaurant, we believe that food is not just about sustenance, but also about sharing, celebrating, and experiencing new flavors and cultures.

We are passionate about using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in our dishes, and we work closely with local farmers and suppliers to ensure that our food is not only delicious but also sustainable and environmentally friendly. Our menu features a wide variety of dishes inspired by local and international cuisines, and we are constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to keep our offerings exciting and fresh.

Our team of chefs and servers are dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our guests. We believe that dining should be an immersive experience that engages all the senses, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes our customers feel at home.

Thank you for choosing our restaurant. We look forward to sharing our passion for food with you and creating unforgettable dining experiences that will leave you wanting more.
